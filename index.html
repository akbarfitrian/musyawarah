Marketplace

Any post can become a hire.

Turn a post into a listing, negotiate the price by DM, and settle the whole job — delivery, confirmation, and payment — without leaving the app.

01

List

Toggle "Post skill listing" on any post — title, category, price, per-task or subscription. It shows up in the feed with a price badge.

02

Negotiate

"Negotiate & Hire" opens a DM with the listing attached. Either side proposes a price as an offer card — accept or decline, or it auto-expires after 3 hours unanswered.

03

Escrow

Buyer locks payment to a treasury wallet. Both sides get a reminder if it sits waiting — starting at 1 hour, then every 6 hours.

04

Deliver

Provider drops a link to the finished work — Drive, GitHub, Figma, wherever it lives. The buyer's confirm button only lights up once it's there.

05

Confirm or dispute

Buyer confirms, or disputes once with a reason if the work isn't right — the provider can answer with a revised link, which reopens confirmation. A provider who never delivers gets auto-disputed, then auto-flagged for a refund if they stay silent.

06

Review

Once released, both sides leave a star rating. Ratings roll up into a reputation badge on the provider's profile.

Trust model: escrow is custodial, not trustless — the same treasury wallet used for verification payments holds locked funds until release. Nothing gets stuck: if a provider never delivers, the order is auto-flagged for dispute after 24 hours, then flagged for an operator refund after another 24 hours of silence; if a buyer never confirms a delivered order, it auto-completes after 72 hours.

How it works

From connect to hired, in one app.

No forms. Everything below runs off the same wallet you connect with.

01

Connect wallet

Your Sphere wallet is detected automatically, or connect via popup within seconds. That address is your whole profile — no email, no password.

02

Post, tip, get verified

Post, reply, and repost — quota and character limit follow your verification tier. Tip a good opinion straight to the author's wallet, and work through quests as you go.

03

Hire, or get hired

Turn any post into a listing, negotiate a price by DM, then lock payment in escrow. Delivery needs a link, confirmation unlocks it, and a dispute or no-show is auto-resolved so nothing sits stuck.

04

Build a reputation

Once a job's released, both sides leave a star rating that rolls up into a reputation badge — visible on the profile the next time someone's deciding whether to hire you.
